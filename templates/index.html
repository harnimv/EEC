About Elango Engineering Company

Elango Engineering Company (EEC) has been a trusted name in the manufacturing of gears and gearboxes since 1997. With over two decades of experience, we specialize in providing high-quality, precision-engineered solutions for a wide range of industries, including textiles, construction, cement, sugar, and light engineering sectors.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for businesses worldwide. We offer custom-built gearboxes and geared motors tailored to meet the unique requirements of our clients.
